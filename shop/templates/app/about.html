About Us


Welcome to BS Mart, your one-stop-shop for all your daily needs! As the name suggests, BS Mart is named after the founders Bhima Shankar , who has been in the retail industry for over two decades. Conveniently located in the bustling area of Sapna Sangeeta in Indore, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the best products at the most competitive prices. At BS Mart, we believe that quality should not be compromised for affordability. That's why we offer a wide range of products that cater to all your needs, including groceries, fresh produce, household items, personal care products, and more. Our shelves are stocked with top brands and high-quality products that are guaranteed to meet your expectations. We understand the value of your time, which is why we make shopping with us a hassle-free experience. Our store is well-organized, making it easy for you to find what you need quickly. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always on hand to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. At BS Mart, we take pride in providing excellent customer service and building long-lasting relationships with our customers. We are committed to delivering the best shopping experience possible, and we are constantly striving to improve our services to meet your needs. So whether you're looking for fresh produce, household essentials, or the latest beauty products, you can trust BS Mart to provide you with everything you need. Come visit us today and experience the BS Mart difference!
